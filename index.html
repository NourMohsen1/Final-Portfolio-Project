Hello, Welcome

Hello, I'm Nour Mohsen, a dedicated student currently pursuing a degree in Communication Design at the New York City of Technology. My academic journey includes an Associate Degree in Industrial Design Technology, providing me with a strong foundation in the field. Proficient in Solidworks mechanical design and a certified user, I possess a versatile skill set encompassing software such as Solidworks, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Adobe After Effects, and KeyShot.

Being bilingual in English and Arabic allows me to communicate effectively in diverse environments. Beyond my academic and linguistic capabilities, I bring practical experience in various aspects of design, including general knowledge in Arduino and Arduino IDE. Additionally, I am well-versed in utilizing 3D printers, laser cutting machines, and woodworking tools to bring creative concepts to life. With a passion for both Graphic and Industrial Design, I am committed to continuous learning and pushing creative boundaries in the ever-evolving design landscape.

REACHE Logo

02

For this project, I was tasked with designing a cover for PRINT Magazine, a leading publication in the graphic design industry with over 80 years of influence. The objective was to create a cover that appeals to a new generation of graphic designers while reflecting my personal expression and the community I identify with.Given the freedom to choose any subject for the cover, I selected sports, focusing on football and, specifically, Lionel Messi. As one of the most iconic figures in football, Messi represents excellence and inspiration, making him a fitting subject for this project. I incorporated imagery of Messi alongside some brief text about his achievements to complete the design.​​​​​​​
